## Purpose
A tool to force me to think about how I am spending time, and either force me to actively do a restful activity or get back to work instead of getting stuck watching youtube.
## Ambitions
Code a good app
- a good project to learn android now that I have some time
- I can have it be however I want
- I can export the data as a csv to do data analysis when the initial objective is achieved.
Features:
- always ticking along a task I am doing. For the tasks, I want them to reflect an underlying motivation instead of the action being performed. Not studying, obligated working or something like that.
    - sleeping
    - Unproductive - limbo (nothing to do, overwhelmed, between activities)
    - Unproductive - resting (Youtube)
    - Unproductive - procrastinating
    - Productive - sleeping
    - Productive - resting (Reading)
    - Productive - social (sittning, hangout, video games)
    - Productive - unobligated (free time project)
    - Productive - obligated (deadlined task)
- Breakpoints in the evening, with evaluations of how I felt during the day, for data analysis
